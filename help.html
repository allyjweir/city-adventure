A new way to explore a city.

We're sorry to hear you are stuck! Here is a quick FAQ

  • What is an adventure? - An adventure is a tour of a city based on a theme. These will take you to different landmarks around a city and give you fun tasks to complete along the way
  • What do I do at a Landmark? - Take a look around! City Adventures takes you to some of the coolest places in the city. Explore and find the amazing history at each of them. When you are ready, complete the quiz and meet up with some other adventurers with the Landmark Chat Rooms!
  • These don't answer my question, what next? - Tweet us @CityAdventuresHelp and we will try and help out. :)

If you are still having problems, drop us an email at help@cityadventures.com
