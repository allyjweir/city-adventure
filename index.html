A new way to explore a city.

Take one of our handmade adventure trails through a city and discover a new side to your destination. Find hidden gems, old classics and answer fun quizzes as you learn the history around you.

City Adventure can introduce you to a new city and give you a local's perspective. Get more out of your trip based on our themed adventure trails to find the best places in a city, how to get between them, and have fun while doing it!
